Getting Started

How do I create my first workout?

‚ñº

To create your first workout, tap the "Start a Workout" button on the Workouts tab. You can choose from trainer-generated templates or create your own custom workout. Select exercises, set your reps and weights, and you're ready to go!

How do I track my exercises?

‚ñº

During a workout, tap on any exercise to view its details. Enter your completed reps and weight for each set. The app will automatically track your progress and update your performance data.

How do I view my performance over time?

‚ñº

Navigate to the History tab to see all your completed workouts. Tap on any exercise to view detailed performance graphs showing your progress over time.

Features

What is workout generation?

‚ñº

FitHub can automatically generate personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, available equipment, and goals. The trainer creates optimized workouts that adapt to your progress over time.

How do I customize my equipment?

‚ñº

Go to Settings and select "Equipment" to customize what equipment you have available. This helps FitHub suggest exercises that match your gym setup.

What are workout templates?

‚ñº

Templates are pre-built workout plans that you can save and reuse. You can create custom templates or use trainer-generated ones. Templates can be scheduled for specific dates and will send you reminders.

Troubleshooting

My workout isn't generating

‚ñº

Make sure you have completed the initial setup, including selecting your equipment and entering some performance data. The app needs this information to generate personalized workouts. If you're on the free plan, check if you've reached the generation limit.

My data isn't saving

‚ñº

FitHub automatically saves your data. If you're experiencing issues, try force-closing and reopening the app. Make sure you have sufficient storage space on your device. If problems persist, contact support.

I can't find an exercise

‚ñº

Use the search function in the exercise browser. You can filter by muscle group, equipment, or difficulty level. If you still can't find what you're looking for, you can create a custom exercise in Settings.

Account & Settings

How do I change my profile information?

‚ñº

Go to the Settings tab and tap on "Profile" to update your information including age, weight, height, and fitness goals.

How do I manage my subscription?

‚ñº

Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to manage your FitHub subscription.

How do I export my data?

‚ñº

Your workout data is stored locally on your device. Currently, data export features are being developed. For data backup, ensure you have iCloud backup enabled for your device.
